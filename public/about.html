About

Open Polsia is an open-source autonomous AI system that runs entire companies through coordinated AI agents. It plans, codes, and markets daily while founders sleep.

You create a company with a vision. AI agents — acting as CEO, Engineer, Growth Manager, and more — run on daily cycles, creating and executing tasks autonomously. They write code, deploy to production, send emails, post on social media, and analyse metrics — all without human intervention.

The project is fully open source and available on GitHub.
